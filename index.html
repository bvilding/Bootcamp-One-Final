Maximize your time

Leave the planning and coordinating to us. After the consultation, you’ll be asked to provide the key features to be photographed, and the contact person(s). Once that is recorded, your project will be on it’s way to completion.

Your photos, your rights

All of the photos include digital copies and unlimited rights. You deserve to have access to your photos and use them any way you like. For extra edits or distribution services please contact hq@vinnihoke.com.

How are projects completed?

Step 1

We meet to discuss the scope of work. The photos need to convey your message and really bring the project to life. We’ll discuss your budget, and record all the details.

Step 2

The stage is set, now it’s time to perform. You can either have us coordinate everything, or if you already have a date set, we will log that day.

Project Completed

After editing, your photos will be ready for their final home, wherever that may be. There are a few options of delivery, but you can decide what works best for you. We want you to love your images, so if we can assist you in anyway after the shoot, please let us know!
